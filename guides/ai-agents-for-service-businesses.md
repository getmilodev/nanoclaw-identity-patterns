## What Are AI Agents (And What They're Not)

AI agents are **not chatbots**. Chatbots follow scripts. When the script breaks, the customer gets frustrated.

AI agents are autonomous software that:
- **Understand context** — "My basement is flooding" gets treated differently than "I need a quote for a kitchen remodel"
- **Take action** — Book appointments, dispatch emergency techs, send confirmation texts
- **Learn your business** — Trained on your services, pricing, availability, and brand voice
- **Work 24/7** — No sick days, no lunch breaks, no hold music

## The 3-Agent Team Model

Most service businesses need exactly three agents working together:

### Agent 1: The AI Receptionist
**Job:** Answer every call, text, and web inquiry instantly.

- Picks up on the first ring, 24/7/365
- Qualifies the inquiry (emergency vs. routine, residential vs. commercial)
- Books appointments directly into your calendar
- Handles FAQs (pricing, hours, service area, availability)
- Sounds like your best employee, not a robot

**ROI example:** A plumbing company missing 5 calls/week at $350 average ticket = $7,000/month in recovered revenue.

### Agent 2: The Follow-Up Agent
**Job:** Re-engage every quote and lead that went cold.

- Follows up on estimates at 24h, 48h, and 7 days
- Sends personalized texts: "Hey [name], just checking if you had questions about the estimate for [address]"
- Reactivates past customers for seasonal services
- Responds to web form submissions within 2 minutes (speed-to-lead)

**ROI example:** A roofing company converting just 2 additional estimates/month at $12,000 average = $24,000/month.

### Agent 3: The Reputation Builder
**Job:** Turn happy customers into 5-star reviews and referrals.

- Sends review requests after every completed job
- Times the ask perfectly (right after positive service experience)
- Manages your Google Business profile reputation
- Runs referral incentive programs automatically

**ROI example:** Moving from 4.2 to 4.7 stars on Google increases click-through rate by 25%.

## Industry-Specific Blueprints

### Plumbing & HVAC
- **Emergency triage:** AI asks about water location, shutoff valve status, severity — dispatches on-call tech for real emergencies
- **Multi-trade routing:** Plumbing vs. HVAC vs. electrical calls get different qualification flows
- **Seasonal campaigns:** Spring AC tune-ups, fall furnace prep, winter pipe winterization
- **Maintenance contracts:** Converts one-time customers into recurring service plans

### Pest Control
- **Panic call handling:** Bed bugs, rats, termites — AI provides immediate reassurance while booking same-day service
- **Seasonal outreach:** Automatic campaigns when ant/mosquito/termite season hits
- **Recurring service scheduler:** Converts one-time treatments into quarterly plans
- **Photo intake:** Requests pest photos via text for pre-visit assessment

### Roofing & Restoration
- **Storm surge management:** Handles 10x normal call volume after weather events
- **Insurance claim triage:** Separates insurance claims from direct purchase inquiries
- **High-value estimate follow-up:** $8K-25K quotes get persistent, professional follow-up
- **Inspection scheduling:** Books roof inspections with minimal back-and-forth

### Property Management
- **Tenant maintenance intake:** Logs issues, assesses urgency, dispatches vendors
- **Showing scheduler:** Books prospective tenants into available time slots
- **Owner updates:** Automated monthly property reports
- **Lease renewal automation:** Proactive outreach 60 days before expiry

### Moving Companies
- **Quote capture:** Origin/destination, move date, inventory, special items — all captured instantly
- **Speed-to-lead:** Responds to web inquiries within 2 minutes
- **Booking confirmation:** Reduces no-shows with automated confirmations and reminders

### Medical Spas
- **Treatment consultation:** Answers questions about procedures, pricing, recovery
- **Appointment optimization:** Fills cancellations from waitlist automatically
- **Client retention:** Detects lapsed clients and re-engages them

### Garage Door Repair
- **Emergency dispatch:** "My garage door won't open" gets immediate response and tech dispatch
- **Diagnostic questions:** AI identifies likely issue before tech arrives, improving first-visit fix rates
